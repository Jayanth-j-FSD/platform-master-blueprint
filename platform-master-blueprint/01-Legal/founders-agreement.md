- Identity and verification systems  
- Social networking layers  
- Marketplace infrastructure  
- Collaboration tools  
- Integrated payments ecosystem  

All technology, code, design, business models, trade secrets, and documentation created under this project belong to the Company.

## 4. Vesting Schedule (Mandatory)

To protect the Company and prevent walkaways:

- **4-year vesting**
- **1-year cliff**
- Monthly vesting after the 12th month

If a Founder leaves before the cliff, **they receive 0% equity**.

If a Founder leaves after the cliff, they only keep the vested portion.

Unvested equity returns to the Company.

---

## 5. Intellectual Property Ownership

All intellectual property (“IP”) created by any Founder:

- Code  
- Algorithms  
- Architecture  
- UI/UX  
- Documents  
- Business logic  
- Databases  
- Names, branding  
- Patents/trademarks  
- Research  
- Tools, scripts, infra configurations  

**is fully assigned to the Company immediately upon creation.**

No Founder retains personal rights over Company IP.

A separate **IP Assignment Agreement** will also be signed.

---

## 6. Decision-Making & Voting

Decisions fall into two categories:

### **A. Day-to-Day Product Decisions**
Handled by responsible founders based on their domain.

### **B. Major Decisions (Require Majority or Unanimous Vote):**
- Changing equity structure  
- Issuing shares  
- Bringing new founders  
- Raising investment  
- Selling the company  
- Changing core product direction  
- Legal disputes  
- Major hires  
- Platform’s security policies  
- IP licensing to external parties  

Voting Model: **Majority = >50% equity vote.**

But critical decisions must be **unanimous**.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

All Founders agree NOT to reveal:

- Platform architecture  
- Identity graph logic  
- Marketplace algorithms  
- Business model details  
- All internal documents  
- Roadmaps, financials  
- Source code, repositories  
- Private discussions between founders  

This obligation survives even if a Founder leaves.

---

## 8. Founder Exit or Removal

If a Founder:

- fails to contribute  
- acts against the Company  
- leaks confidential information  
- commits fraud  
- behaves unethically  
- disrupts operations  

The remaining founders may **vote for removal**.

Removed founder keeps only vested equity.  
Unvested equity returns to the Company pool.

---

## 9. Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation

For **3 years after leaving**, a Founder may NOT:

- build or join a competing platform  
- solicit investors or employees of the Company  
- use Company data, code, or designs  
- develop a clone or derivative product  

This is critical given the confidential nature of the platform.

---

## 10. Capital Contribution

Founders agree to the following:

- Initial expenses are covered through mutual agreement.
- Investments made by founders must be recorded.
- Reimbursements happen only after funding or revenue.

No Founder is forced to invest money unless agreed.
